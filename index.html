Vintage Modular Synth — Listener Study

~9 minutes · headphones recommended

The task

You will hear 22 short audio clips (~30 seconds each, around 11 minutes total). For each clip you'll answer in two steps:

  • Step 1: Which synth family does it sound like? (Buchla, Serge, EMS/VCS3, Soundfreak — or "modern/digital" or "I don't recognise")
  • Step 2: If you picked a family, which specific module within that family? (a list of options appears)

Each tap locks in your answer and the next clip loads automatically. There are no right or wrong answers — your perception is the data. The two-step format lets us measure family-level vs module-level recognition separately. A few clips are deliberate sanity-check controls.

Before you start

  • Use headphones if you have them. Laptop speakers also work.
  • Set your volume to a comfortable level using the warm-up clip below.
  • If you don't recognise a particular sound, just pick "I don't recognise" — that's useful data too.
